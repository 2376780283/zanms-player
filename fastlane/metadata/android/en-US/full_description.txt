<h3>Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Play music from Bilibili, YouTube, MusicFree plugin, local files, and AList</li>
<li>Playlist subscription</li>
<li>Extract song name and search lyric</li>
<li>Change theme and custom theme</li>
<li>Playlist cloud backup and sync with Azusa Player extension</li>
<li>Login on Bilibili and interact with video</li>
<li>Multilingual</li>
<li>Cache songs</li>
<li>Support Android Auto, CarPlay</li>
<li>Volume equalization with FFmpeg</li>
<li>AB loop playback</li>
<li>Word-by-word lyrics</li>
</ul>
